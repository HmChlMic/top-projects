Mixed Veggies and Fruit Salad

Want some fiber in your diet? Try this recipe!

Ingredients

  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Seedless Grapes
  • Fresh Basil
  • Lettuce
  • Strawberries
  • Sunflower Seeds
  • Cucumber
  • Olive Oil
  • Sunflower Seeds
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Chop the cucumber into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Clean the red bell pepper, removing the seeds, and then chop into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Trim and slice the strawberries.
  4. Cut the grapes in half (makes it easier to eat them with a fork).
  5. Chop the fresh basil.
  6. Place all the fruits and vegetables in a large bowl and add in the sunflower seeds.
  7. Juice the lime and combine with the olive oil and salt.
  8. Pour the dressing into the bowl.
  9. Toss to coat.
  10. Serve this green salad with fruit on large, fresh, crisp lettuce leaves.
