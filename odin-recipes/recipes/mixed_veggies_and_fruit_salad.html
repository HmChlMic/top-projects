Chicken, Egg, and Lettuce Sandwhich

A balanced meal rich in protein, carbohydrates, and fiber

Macros

According to fitia a serving (~220g) of a chicken and egg sandwhich, which consists of 75g of chicken, 1 leaf (10g) of lettuce, 2 slices (80g) of bread, and 1 (55g) count egg contains the following macros:

  • Calories: 377 kcal
  • Protein: 32.0 g
  • Carbohydrates 38.0 g
  • Fats: 10.0 g

In tracking your macros, be sure to adjust it based on your serving size. Do your own research on macros of individual ingredients if necessary i.e. changing amounts of ingredients :D

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Egg
  • Sandwhich
  • Lettuce
