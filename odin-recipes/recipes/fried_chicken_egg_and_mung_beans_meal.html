Fried Chicken and Mung Beans Meal

A balanced meal rich in protein, carbohydrates, and fiber

Macros

Using the data in spe.lt that shows the macros of chicken and mung beans, nutritionix which displays the macros of rice, and University of Rochester Medical Center

that shows the macros of rice, we could say that the fried chicken, egg, and mung beans meal have the following macros:
  • Calories: 662 kcal
  • Protein: 45.0 g
  • Carbohydrates 75 g
  • Fats: 20.0 g

In tracking your macros, be sure to adjust it based on your serving size. Do your own research on macros of individual ingredients if necessary i.e. changing amounts of ingredients :D

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Egg
  • Rice
  • Mung beans

Steps

  1. Rinse, and then boil mung beans for a long while until it softens
  2. Fry chicken
  3. Cook Rice
  4. Eat and Track ur food :D
